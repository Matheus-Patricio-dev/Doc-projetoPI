What is

NEOORBIT?

NeoOrbit® was born in 2018 with a clear mission: to transform the way companies access and use satellite technology. Since the beginning, our company has specialized in the sale of services and private satellites, offering large corporations the possibility of owning their own constellations. We take care of the entire launch process, ensuring the delivery of high performance and reliable solutions.

Today, in 2025, we are the largest and most innovative company in the aerospace sector, with a portfolio that serves the largest companies in the world. The quality, security and expertise we deliver have made NeoOrbit the main choice for corporations looking for next-generation satellite solutions, solidifying our leadership position in the global market.
